### 2.1 Addressing Colleagues (Anrede)

<div className="info-box">
  <h4>Formal vs. Informal Address</h4>
  
  **Formal (Sie)**
  - With supervisors
  - With new colleagues
  - In meetings
  - With clients
  - In written communication

  **Informal (du)**
  - With close colleagues
  - After being invited to use "du"
  - In casual team settings

## Cultural Notes

- Germans typically use formal language (Sie) in business settings until invited to use informal language (du)
- Punctuality is extremely important in German business culture
- Written communication tends to be more formal and structured than in some other cultures
- Office hierarchies are usually clearly defined and respected
